Anti-Phishing Awareness

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where scammers try to trick you into giving personal information, such as passwords or credit card details, by pretending to be a trusted entity.

How to Stay Safe

  • Check URLs carefully before entering sensitive information.
  • Do not click on suspicious email links.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) whenever possible.
  • Use an anti-phishing browser extension.
